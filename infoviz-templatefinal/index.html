About Our Tool

This interactive visualization allows a user (you) to better understand how students feel throughout various phases of a typical capstone course. For the purposes of this visualization, the data was taken from the "Inspire" program at the University of Victoria, Canada. In this capstone project, software engineering students worked alongside a local community partner in Victoria to ideate and implement software that would benefit our community. Students faced and overcame many challenges, and this visualization demonstrates how this impacted their emotions. By selecting the emotion(s) of interest, you can see the trend of this emotion throughout a 16 week semester, and by clicking on a particular point of interest, you can see causes of this emotion as well as strategies on how to manage the emotion.
